Meet the Team

Whether they're conservationists out in the field or campaign strategists marshalling communities of supporters, WWF-Australia is made up of dedicated people who are passionate about saving the species and places we love.

The team works across four priority areas - marine, species, food and climate. Our conservation goals for these areas are driven by some of Australia's best minds in science, policy, business and communications. It's not just traditional conservation work they're doing, our team focuses on the causes of the issues as well.
